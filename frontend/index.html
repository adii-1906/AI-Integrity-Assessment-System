§ IVIntegrity Index Formulation

Let S = {S₁, S₂, S₃, S₄, S₅} denote the normalized scores (∈ [0,100]) from modules M₁–M₅. The Integrity Index (II) uses weighted harmonic mean — penalizing any single-dimension failure.

II = Σwᵢ / Σ(wᵢ / Sᵢ)
WHERE: Σwᵢ = 1.0 · wᵢ ∈ {0.20, 0.25, 0.20, 0.15, 0.20} · w₂(Hallucination) is highest due to factual primacy

Trust tiers: High Trust (II ≥ 85) · Moderate Trust (65–84) · Low Trust (40–64) · Reject (<40).
